## üí° QUICK TIPS

**Description Optimization:**

- Update every 3-6 months with new services/results
- A/B test different CTAs (audit vs. consultation vs. free strategy call)
- Add seasonal offers (e.g., "BFCM Email Audit - Free in November")

**Photo Strategy:**

- Upload new photos weekly (keeps profile fresh in Google's algorithm)
- Show faces when possible (photos with people get 42% more engagement)
- Use action shots over static product images
- Add photos of remote work setups (builds trust for online-only business)

**Review Generation:**

- Ask within 48 hours of project completion (strike while iron is hot)
- Make it ridiculously easy (direct link, mobile-friendly)
- Offer incentive without quid pro quo: "If you leave a review, I'll review your business too"
- Never offer discounts/payments for reviews (against Google TOS)

**Google Posts:**

- Post every 7-10 days (algorithm favors active profiles)
- Use high-quality images (1200x900px minimum)
- Include clear CTA in every post
- Add relevant hashtags (3-5 max)
- Repurpose blog content, case studies, client wins

**Q&A Management:**

- Check Q&A section weekly for new questions
- Respond within 24 hours
- Pre-populate with questions customers actually ask
- Update answers when services/pricing change
